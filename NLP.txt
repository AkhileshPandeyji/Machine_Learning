NLP - Natural Language Processing - Text Processing
nltk - natural language toolkit

5 steps to follow:

1.Tokenization - nltk.tokenize - word_tokenize , sent_tokenize
2.Removing Stopwords - nltk.corpus - stopwords
3.Stemming and Lemmatization - nltk.stem - PorterStemmer,WordNetLemmatizer
4.Vectorization - sklearn.feature_extraction.text - CountVectorizer,TfidVectorizer
5.TF-IDF Transformation - sklearn.feature_extraction.text - TfidfTransformer
